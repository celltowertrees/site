Weird Projects

E-METER

At ITP camp 2017 I attempted to recreate the "stress detector test" ("e-meter") used by Scientologists to attempt to convert people to their church. It's powered by Arduino and uses a heart rate monitor on one finger and a skin conductance monitor for the other two. The dial is supposedly from a real e-meter, the rest was constructed by me with help and support from some wonderful ITP camp counselors.

Arduino, some kind of compressed board, plastic dial, iridescent acrylic, heart sensor, skin conductance sensor
